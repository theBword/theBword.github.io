01 — The Strategist

I don't just write content. I design the systems that make content work.

I specialize in helping brands close the gap between what they offer and how their audience actually experiences that value. My work sits at the intersection of content strategy, audience psychology, and brand voice — translating complex ideas into communication that feels personal, trustworthy, and human.

From 10 years building community strategy at AARP to lifecycle campaign leadership at PenFed Credit Union, I've delivered measurable results across regulated environments, mission-driven organizations, and consumer-facing brands.
